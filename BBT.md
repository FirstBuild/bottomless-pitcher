####BBT Test Plan - Autofill Pitcher
-----------------

|Test ID|Test Description|Initial Condition|Test Procedure|Expected Results|
| ---|---|---|---|---|
|normal_use_empty|single time typical usage of the feature|empty pitcher (lid right side up), pitcher undocked,power on, software running|dock pitcher lid, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds, remove pitcher lid|5 second delay once pitcher is docked, then 120VAC output will be live, 120VAC turns off once pitcher lid is removed from the dock|||
|normal_use_full|single time typical usage of the feature|full pitcher (lid upside down), pitcher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should not go live at any point in time|||
|power_outage_standby|power outage during standby |no pitcher, power on, software running|with pitcher undocked, cycle power to unit, onitor 120VAC output for 30 seconds|120VAC output should not go live at any point in time|||
|power_outage_filling|power outage during filling|empty pitcher (lid right side up), picher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid and verify 120VAC is live after 5 seconds, cycle power, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should turn off when power is turned off, then back on when power is returned and software restarted (up to 15 seconds)|||
|power_outage_full|power outage when full|full pitcher (lid upside down), picher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid and verify 120VAC does not go live, cycle power, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should not go live at any point in time|||
|quick_replacement_filling(2)|quick pitcher replacement during filling state - repeat 2 times|empty pitcher (lid right side up), picher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid and verify 120VAC is live after 5 seconds, remove pitcher lid and replace quickly (within 1 second), repeat 2 times, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds after final replacement, remove pitcher lid |120VAC output should go live within 5 seconds of the final replacement, 120 VAC turns off once pitcher lid is removed from the dock|||
|quick_replacement_filling(5)|quick pitcher replacement during filling state - repeat 5 times|empty pitcher (lid right side up), picher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid and verify 120VAC is live after 5 seconds, remove pitcher lid and replace quickly (within 1 second), repeat 5 times, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds after final replacement, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should go live within 5 seconds of the final replacement, 120 VAC turns off once pitcher lid is removed from the dock|||
|quick_replacement_full(2)|quick pitcher replacement during full state - repeat 2 times|full pitcher (lid upside down), pitcher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid and verify 120VAC does not go live after 10 seconds, remove pitcher lid and replace quickly (within 1 second), repeat 2 times, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds after final replacement, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should not go live at any point in time|||
|quick_replacement_full(5)|quick pitcher replacement during full state - repeat 5 times|full pitcher (lid upside down), pitcher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid and verify 120VAC does not go live after 10 seconds, remove pitcher lid and replace quickly (within 1 second), repeat 5 times, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds after final replacement, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should not go live at any point in time|||
|long_term_usage_light|extended light usage - one time per day|empty pitcher (lid right side up), picher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds, remove pitcher lid, repeat once per day for 30 days|5 second delay once pitcher is docked, then 120VAC output will be live, 120VAC turns off once pitcher lid is removed from the dock|||
|long_term_usage_heavy|extended heavy usage - 10 times per day|empty pitcher (lid right side up), picher undocked, power on, software running|dock pitcher lid, monitor 120VAC output for 15 seconds, remove pitcher lid, repeat 5 times per day for 30 days|5 second delay once pitcher is docked, then 120VAC output will be live, 120VAC turns off once pitcher lid is removed from the dock|||
|power_on_empty|initial power-up with pitcher installed and empty|empty pitcher (lid right side up), pitcher docked, power off, software not running|turn on power supply to device, wait 15 seconds, monitor 120VAC output, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should go live, then turn off when the pitcher lid is removed from the dock|||
|power_on_full|initial power-up with pitcher installed and full|full pitcher (lid upside down), pitcher docked, power off, software not running|turn on power supply to device, wait 15 seconds, monitor 120VAC output, remove pitcher lid|120VAC output should not go live at any point in time|||
|power_on_no_pitcher|initial power-up with no pitcher|no pitcher lid, power off, software not running|turn on power supply to device, wait 15 seconds, monitor 120VAC output|120VAC output should not go live at any point in time|||
